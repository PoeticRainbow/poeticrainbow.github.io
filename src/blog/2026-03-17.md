## New
- Froglights have been retextured and renamed to Magmalights
- Raw Ore Blocks and Items have been fully redesigned
- Copper Grates have been given new grid-like textures
- Retextured Bamboo Planks to resemble early Classic planks
## Options
- Added two options for Bamboo Planks: consistent and smooth
## Changes
- Wildflowers are now Yellow and White instead of Yellow and Red
- Flowerbed blocks no longer have UV rotation (Pink Petals, Leaf Litter, etc.)
- Leaf Litter now resemble Birch Leaves in color
## Fix
- Use Forge OBJ loader on 1.20.1 and NeoForge on 1.20.2+
- Fix translucency on Cherry, Mangrove, and Azalea leaves showing in 26.1
- Fix Chiseled Tuff tops not matching the sides
