A whole bunch of new textures! And a slightly new versioning scheme.

![A picture of a cave containing many of the blocks mentioned in the changelog](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/37235801-3d94-4dd6-afdf-dcdc6a7fb839)

## New
- Respawn Anchor texture inspired by Crying Obsidian and Lapis
- Sulfur & Cinnabar block sets
- Beta repeater block texture
- Gear easter egg

## Changes
- New Andesite, Diorite, and Granite textures
- New Packed Mud & Mud Bricks
- Reshaded Coral Blocks, new Tube Coral textures
- New Lodestone textures
- Changes to Concrete and Concrete Powder for variation (lighter Powder, darker Concrete)
- Tuff block set tweaks to help resemble Stone Bricks more

## Fixes
- Bed tops wrong texture orientation
- Bats having no hanging animation
- Shield texture not showing
- Potted Fern having different lighting
- Bed models missing texture (26.2)
- Decorated Pot invisible in inventory (<1.21.4)

**Full Changelog**: [https://github.com/PoeticRainbow/golden-days/compare/1.15.6...16.0](https://github.com/PoeticRainbow/golden-days/compare/1.15.6...16.0)
